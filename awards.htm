Whitney Houston holds the Guinness World Record as the most awarded female artist of all time, with more than 400 career awards. She won:

  • 2 Emmy Awards
  • 6 Grammy Awards
  • 30 Billboard Music Awards
  • 22 American Music Awards
  • All-time record for the most American Music Awards of any female solo artist
  • 8 wins in AMAs 1994
  • 11 Billboard Music Awards 1993
  • Most WMAs won in a single year
  • 5 awards at the 6th World Music Awards in 1994
